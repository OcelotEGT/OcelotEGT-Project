What are eSports?

eSports stands for Electronic Sports and they are videogame competitions that have become highly popular events.
In general, eSports are multiplayer videogames competitions, particularly among professional players.
The most common genres in videogames associated with sports are: real-time strategy, first-person shooter and online multiplayer battle arenas (MOBA)
